Our Story

A Legacy of Taste Since 2010

From a single spark of culinary passion in Athani, Ernakulam, a legacy was born. In 2010, AL-REEM MANDI introduced Kerala to the true essence of Arabian Mandi, becoming one of the very first to share this rich heritage. Our journey, which started from that single location, has since expanded to a constellation of over 20 restaurants across the state.

We are committed to preserving the authenticity of Arabian flavors, blending tradition with innovation in every dish we serve. Our chefs are passionate artisans, dedicated to sourcing the finest ingredients and perfecting every recipe.

Today, Al-Reem Mandi is not just a restaurant—it's a community of food lovers, a destination for families and friends, and a symbol of quality and hospitality across Kerala and beyond.

From humble beginnings to a growing family of outlets, our story is shaped by the trust of our guests and the dedication of our team. We continue to evolve while staying true to our roots—serving meals that are memorable, comforting, and genuinely Arabian.

Whether it's your first visit or your hundredth, we’re honored to welcome you to the Al-Reem experience—where warmth, flavor, and tradition meet.
